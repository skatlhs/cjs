    • Goal Setting

    You need to have very clear short-term, midterm, and long-range goals. Peak Performers set out to turn their dreams into reality by taking action through goal setting... Mental athletes are goal-oriented. They have vision. When an athlete complains of lacking motivation, you can be sure that it’s almost always caused by goals that fail to inspire him to action. Goals serve to keep you on target. They increase the desire to achieve.

    Visualization or Mental Rehearsal

    Peak Performers must practice seeing themselves training successfully in their mind. Athletes, who visualize how to handle training situations and competition scenarios successfully tend to be far more successful in actual practice.

    Self Talk

    The average person thinks 50,000 thoughts a day. If the majority of that self-talk is negative then performance will be affected. The first step in optimizing self-talk is to recognize that it is there and then working on challenging the negative words and beliefs.

    Breathing/Arousal Control

    Becoming aware of our breathing and taking control of it when we need to is one of the most powerful ways to take control of ourselves, especially when we’re about to compete. Creating the wiring in our brain to calm ourselves in a stressful situation will help us make more effective choices, be less reactive and ultimately help us to survive the situation as best we can.

    What Does Going Keto Mean?

    Going keto means that your body burns ketones as fuel rather than glucose.

    What's a Ketone?

    A ketone is a kind of molecule, a kind of molecule we humans make from fat. Ketones get shipped to mitochondrial machines bathing in oxygen found inside our cells. Once in there, ketones get oxidized to generates ATP. Chemically speaking, the central feature of a ketone is its 'parent structure', the double-bond between a carbon atom and an oxygen atom. From this structure, two other carbon-contain chemical groups branching off of it.

    Benefits of the Keto for Peak Performance Training Program


    It Burns Body Fat

    Ketones in luence the hormones that control your hunger. Hunger feels different when you are in full ketosis – it fades from a pressing need to a background thought. In full keto, it's very easy to fast, and during the fast, your body is burning up your fat stores for energy. As a Bonus getting that fat out of the way accelerates your ability to add lean muscle.

    It's Anti-Inflamatory

    Burning fat for fuel creates far less in lammation than burning sugar does and ketones themselves turn off in lammatory pathways. Football is a warrior sport where recovery can often take days. Going keto reduces recovery time giving you the opportunity to train harder and more ef iciently during the season.

    It Builds a Stronger Brain

    The higher the level of Football the more the difference is Mental between you and the competition. Ketosis causes your brain to create more neurons. This gives you access to willpower and a sharper mental state when the game is on the line.
